Inscriptions tokens

Inscription tokens constitute a multifaceted innovation within the blockchain and cryptocurrency domain. Primarily, they involve the integration of diverse data directly into blockchain transactions, analogous to the process of inscribing messages onto stone tablets. This mechanism transcends the limitations of a specific blockchain, allowing for the creation of native NFTs, the establishment of digital time capsules, and the provision of decentralized data storage.
